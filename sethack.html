Set Up Your PC For Hacking

Want a good computer for hacking? Hint: don't buy a new one. Chances are, you have an old laptop sitting around somewhere, right? If you don't, then you should buy a cheap laptop now. Got that done? Good. Now follow these simple steps below.

    You'll need...
  • A Laptop
  • Your Favorite Linux ISO (I Recommend Fedora or Redhat. DO NOT USE KALI if you do you are AUTISTIC)
  • A Usb With AT LEAST EIGHT GB of Space
  • A Bootable USB Creator (Link In Step Where It Is Needed)
I will use UUI to install Ubuntu GNOME
  1. Get on the designated laptop
  2. Install UUI
  3. Download Ubuntu GNOME
  4. Plug in your USB drive into the PC
  5. Open up UUI
  6. Follow the instructions
  7. Restart your PC and press one of these key combos: f12 or fn + f12, esc, f2.
  8. You should be in the BIOS menu. If you are there and your USB doesn't show up, you can learn to fix it here
  9. Now, boot up Ubuntu GNOME and click install.
  10. This will install Ubuntu GNOME on your PC
  11. Most of you know enough to have a general idea of what to do, but I'll put in a .src file with the commands to install basic tools.
    12. Download Setup.src here

    You can modify this in any way you like! I have just configured it with the basic things for an OS.
