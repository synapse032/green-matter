Want to meet other hackers? Get online, then! How? Just follow these simple steps.

  1. Download and extract demonsaw.
  2. Run this in the terminal in the newly extracted folder: 

     torsocks [starting directory]/demonsaw_nix_64/Demonsaw/

  3. Now you should have demonsaw open. In the router field, put .onion and put 1888 as the port.
  4. Boom! Connect to server after choosing your nick.
